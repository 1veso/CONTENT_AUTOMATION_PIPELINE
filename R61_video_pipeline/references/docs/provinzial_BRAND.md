# Provinzial — Brand Voice & Style Guide
# Source: Design-Richtlinien für Social-Media-Kanäle im Provinzial Konzern (Februar 2025)

## Who We Are
- Regional insurance group (Sparkassen-Finanzgruppe) serving private and business customers in Germany
- Product: Insurance, Vorsorge, and financial security services
- Stage: Established, large enterprise — part of a national financial group

## Target Audience
- **The Person:** Mid-30s homeowner or young family in NRW/North Germany — "Thomas" — stable job, owns a car, renting or just bought a flat, not thinking about insurance until something goes wrong
- **Their Pain:** Uncertainty about whether they're actually covered when it matters. Too many confusing options.
- **Their Desire:** Peace of mind. Knowing they made the right choice without having to become an expert.
- **Where they hang out:** Instagram, Facebook, YouTube

## Tone & Voice
| Attribute  | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| Formality  | Professional but approachable — not corporate stiff, not casual slang |
| Energy     | Calm authority. Reliable. Grounded. |
| Humor      | None or very light — this is insurance, not entertainment |
| Warmth     | Genuinely human. Community-focused. Local roots. |

### Signature Phrases
- Sicherheit. Klarheit. Provinzial.
- Für das, was wirklich zählt.
- Regional verwurzelt. Persönlich.

### What We NEVER Do
- ❌ Flashy hype language or urgency bait ("Jetzt kaufen! Nur heute!")
- ❌ Fear-mongering about disasters or death
- ❌ Memes or ironic tone
- ❌ Accent color #EC672F without explicit client approval per brief
- ❌ Off-brand color combinations

### Logo
- Golden wings ("Goldene Flügel") — the core brand mark
- Available on: black background, green (#005940) background, white background, transparent
- Always bottom-right corner watermark in video/image templates
- Wing color: `#FFD000`

### Aesthetic
- **3 words:** Trustworthy, grounded, clean
- **Photography Style:** Real people, real situations — lifestyle over studio. Authentic German everyday life. Families, homes, cars, community moments.
- **Typography Feel:** Modern sans-serif. Clear hierarchy. No decorative fonts.

### Background Variants for Content
- Dark: pure black (`#000000`) with yellow wings
- Brand: Provinzial green (`#005940`) with yellow wings
- Light: white with yellow wings

## Content Pillars
1. **Sicherheit im Alltag** — Everyday moments where insurance matters (home, car, family)
2. **Vorsorge & Zukunft** — Planning ahead, financial security, life stages
3. **Schaden & Service** — What happens when things go wrong — fast, human, reliable
4. **Regional & Gemeinschaft** — Local roots, NRW identity, community stories
5. **Produktaufklärung** — Simple explainers of coverage, no jargon

## Platform Strategy
- **Primary:** Instagram — lifestyle imagery, short captions, Reels
- **Secondary:** Facebook — slightly longer copy, community engagement
- **Tertiary:** LinkedIn — employer brand, Sparkassen-Finanzgruppe positioning

## Caption Formula
```
[Relatable situation or human moment — 1 line]
[Brand value or reassurance — 1 line]
[CTA — direct and simple]

[1-2 relevant hashtags max]
```

Example:
```
Dein Zuhause. Dein sicherstes Gefühl. 🏡
Wir sind da, wenn es darauf ankommt.
Jetzt informieren → Link in Bio

#Provinzial #Versicherung
```

## Image Prompt Defaults
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 (Stories/Reels primary)
- Style: Warm, natural lighting. Real German everyday environments.
- People: Diverse but relatable — families, couples, individuals 25-55
- Always authentic, never staged-looking stock photo aesthetic
- Color grading: Warm greens and naturals — reference #005940 palette
- Logo watermark: Yellow wings bottom-right corner
